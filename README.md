# Eye Relaxer 2020 

**Your personal assistant for healthy eyes and better focus.**

Eye Relaxer helps you prevent eye strain using the famous **20-20-20 rule**: Every 20 minutes, it reminds you to look away for 20 seconds.

## ‚ú® Key Features

###  Modern Android 15 Design
- **Fresh Look**: Beautiful big cards with Android 15 theme .
- **Dynamic Themes**: Colors adapt to your wallpaper (Material You).

###  Smart & Intelligent
- **Auto-Pause Detection**: Watching a movie on Netflix or YouTube? On a Zoom call? The app detects this and **automatically pauses** the timer so it doesn't disturb you.
- **Dynamic Island Overlay**: A sleek, non-intrusive pill-shaped timer that floats at the top of your screen during breaks.

###  Eye Care Essentials
- **20-20-20 Timer**: Proven method to reduce eye strain.
- **Strict Mode**: Optional screen dimming to force you to take a break.
- **Detailed Stats**: Track how many breaks you've taken today.
